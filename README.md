# SuperStore Sales Analysis

An interactive sales dashboard analysing regional performance, discount impact, and profitability trends across 10,000+ transactions.

## Business Question
Which regions, categories, and customer segments drive the most profit — and where is discounting hurting margins?

## Key Findings
- West region generates the highest revenue but discount rates vary significantly by category
- Technology is the most profitable category; Furniture drags down overall margins
- High discount rates correlate with negative profit in several sub-categories
- Consumer segment accounts for the largest share of orders across all regions
- Identified high-margin, low-visibility brands — products with strong profit margins but disproportionately low sales volume — surfacing underinvested opportunities for targeted promotional spend

## Tech Stack
Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn, Streamlit
